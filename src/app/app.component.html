Song Sort

Welcome to the song sorter!
Once you enter a playlist link below, you will be asked to choose between two songs until we can determine a definitive ranking of each song on the playlist.
Once you are on the sorting page, be sure that you don't go back or refresh the page. You will lose all your progress. Choose your favorite of the two songs displayed and try not to choose the neutral option too often, or your sort won't be as accurate.
