Enter the Spotify URL to the playlist you want to rank

To get the link to a Spotify playlist, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the playlist page on the Spotify app.
  2. Click the "..." located to the left on the screen, in line with the play button. This will open a menu.
  3. From this menu, select "Share" to open a sub-menu.
  4. Click "Copy link to playlist." This will copy a link to the playlist that you will paste into the text box below.
